How the operating model works

You bring the tools. Kabrios provides the structure.

Your scanners, cloud controls, ticketing systems, identity stack, documentation sources, and evidence repositories remain part of the program. Kabrios adds the documentation layer, workflow structure, dashboards, reporting cadence, and active guidance that make those tools operationally useful together.

This is how you reduce coordination drag while still supporting evidence collection, audits, policies, attestations, third-party reviews, remediation, and leadership reporting.
