You keep the tools. Kabrios provides the governed structure.

Your scanners, cloud controls, ticketing systems, identity stack, documentation sources, and evidence repositories remain part of the program. Kabrios adds the workflow structure, reporting cadence, guidance, and accountability layer that makes them operationally useful together.

This is how you reduce coordination drag while still supporting evidence collection, audits, policies, attestations, third-party reviews, remediation, and leadership reporting.
